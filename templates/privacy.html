Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: your name and email address provided during registration.
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data: basic information about how you interact with the app (e.g., pages visited).

We do not collect payment information or any financial account credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service:

  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure.
  • To display your expense history and summaries.
  • To improve app performance and fix issues.

We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We use industry-standard measures to protect it from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. Your expense data is associated with your account and is not accessible to other users.

You may delete your account at any time, which will permanently remove all associated data from our systems.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal data with third-party services. We may use infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) to operate the app, and these providers are bound by confidentiality obligations.

We do not integrate with advertising networks, analytics platforms, or data brokers.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at privacy@spendly.app. We will respond within a reasonable time.
